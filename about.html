About the App

What is MaxFluency?

MaxFluency is your partner in assisting you in effective communication! It includes lessons and quizzes to help you out. Each lesson has a video to aid you if you're no auditory learner ;)

Meet the Developers

MaxFluency wouldn't have come into existence without its awesome team of developers!

Who is Max?

He is our friendly Neighborhood pug that's out to help us on our journey! He is the mascot of our app due to a few reasons.

  1. It was hard to think of a perfect logo representing communication
  2. Dogs are very expressive animals. They may not be able to talk, but they have many ways to express how they feel
  3. Pugs are cute! They have expressive eyes and they're kind of stubborn, but don't really get into much mischief. Also, they're pretty polite

MaxFluency x Showerthoughts

MaxFluency x Showerthoughts is a partnership between the two development teams. Both teams have own quiz type apps. In exchange for aiding Showerthoughts in coding their app, MaxFluency gets to have on tutorial video starring Rence Dela Cruz (from Showerthoughts) as speaker. Anyway, please support both MaxFluency and Showerthoughts
